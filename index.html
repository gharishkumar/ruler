It seems that you have not calibrated. Click on calibrate to calibrate.
Why calibration?

The actual dpi (pixel density) of your screen may be not known to the browser. So, it would be best if you calibrated the screen with a known reference.

Here we use a credit card to do that.

Place a credit card on the screen and adjust its size on the screen.

Adjust size on-screen by pinching with two fingers to zoom in (or) zoom out, (or) using the scroll up or down.
